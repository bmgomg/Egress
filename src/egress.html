A puzzle of gravity & escape
Egress
A box full of blocks and bubbles. One door. Rotate to empty it.
Door
How does the door behave on side walls?
How to Play
Rotate the box to empty it through the door—a gap in one wall, always at a corner.
  • Blocks are heavier than air, bubbles are lighter than air
  • Pieces can only escape through the door on the top or bottom wall.
  • A block on top of one bubble drags it down—they fall together
  • A block on two or more bubbles gets lifted up—they rise together
Clear the box in as few rotations as possible.
